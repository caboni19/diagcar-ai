DZ Nova upgrade summary
==========================

This version adds app/services/ai_diagnostic_engine.py.

What changed:
- The system no longer behaves like a raw CSV lookup.
- The CSV/database cases are transformed into an internal knowledge base.
- User symptoms are normalized across Arabic, Algerian Darija, French, and English.
- Darija expressions such as "تريبل", "ما تجريش", "دخان كحل", "كلاتش", "فيتاس", "ضو" are mapped to diagnostic concepts.
- The engine computes weighted similarity using:
  1) token-set fuzzy matching,
  2) partial matching,
  3) token overlap,
  4) Darija concept bonus,
  5) category bonus,
  6) vehicle-context bonus.
- The answer now includes chat_response, a natural diagnostic answer in the user's language.

Academic description:
The system uses a hybrid AI-based diagnostic approach combining multilingual symptom normalization, Algerian Darija keyword mapping, rule-based inference, weighted fuzzy similarity scoring, and vehicle-context-aware recommendation.
